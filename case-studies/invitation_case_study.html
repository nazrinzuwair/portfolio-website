01 — Overview

Designing for moments that matter.

Personal celebration invitations are one of the most emotionally significant pieces of print design — they set the tone for the entire event and are often kept as keepsakes long after the day itself. Every design decision, from typeface to colour palette to illustration style, needs to match the mood of the occasion and resonate with the person receiving it.

This collection presents two invitation suites designed for personal celebrations. Each suite was developed from a distinct theme, with custom typography pairings, coordinated colour palettes, and illustrative elements that bring the event's character to life. Both are print-ready at A5 and 5×7 inch formats, suitable for professional printing or home printing.

03 — Design Approach

What makes each invitation work.

Good invitation design works on a hierarchy — the event name catches the eye first, the key details (date, time, venue) read second, and the decorative elements frame and reinforce the mood throughout. Every element serves a purpose: nothing is purely decorative if it doesn't also communicate something about the event.

Typography
Each suite uses a script headline font paired with a clean serif or sans for event details — creating contrast between the emotive headline and the practical information below it.
Colour
Palettes were chosen to match the emotional tone of each event — soft watercolour blues for a gentle baby shower; bold pink and red for a joyful, energetic birthday celebration.
Illustration
Custom or curated illustrative elements (teddy & balloons, ladybugs & florals) are placed to frame the text without competing with it — they set the scene without cluttering the information.
Print Readiness
Both designs are exported at A5 / 5×7 inch format, CMYK-compatible colour profile, with sufficient margins and bleed area for professional print production.
