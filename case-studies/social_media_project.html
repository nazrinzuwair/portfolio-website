01 — Overview

Social media that reflects the brand.

Alongside the NR Creativo Solutions website redesign, I designed a series of promotional social media posts for the company's Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn presence. The brief was to create posts that aligned with the new website's visual identity — clean, professional, and technical-feeling — while communicating specific services, promotions, and brand messages clearly to potential clients.

Each post was designed as a standalone piece that works in a feed context — where a viewer sees it out of sequence and in under two seconds decides whether to stop scrolling. That constraint drives every design decision: strong headline, clear value proposition, minimal clutter, consistent brand colour and typography.

The collection is ongoing — new posts are added regularly as NR Creativo launches new services and promotions.

03 — Design Approach

Designed to stop the scroll.

Every post in this collection was designed around one core principle — a viewer in a social media feed makes a stop-or-scroll decision in under two seconds. The design has to earn that pause with a strong visual hierarchy and a clear message before the caption is even read.

Headline First
Every post leads with the strongest possible statement — a bold fact, a direct question, or a compelling offer. The headline is always the largest, highest-contrast element. Supporting text is secondary.
Brand Consistency
NR Creativo's colour palette (purple, dark, white), typography system, and logo placement are consistent across all posts — so any post could be placed next to any other and look like a family.
Single Clear CTA
Each post has one call to action — whether that's "Book a Free Consultation", "Get 20% Off", or "Contact Us Today". Multiple CTAs split attention; one CTA drives action.
Platform Optimised
All posts are produced at 1080×1080px — the standard square format that works across Instagram feed, Facebook feed, and LinkedIn feed without cropping or reformatting.
